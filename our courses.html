What Is SEO and its Benefits?

SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is a digital marketing strategy focused on improving a website's visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs). The goal is to increase the quantity and quality of organic (non-paid) traffic to a website. SEO involves optimizing various aspects of a website.SEO involves optimizing various aspects of a website, such as its content, structure, and technical elements, to make it more attractive to search engines like Google.

Increased Organic Traffic: Effective SEO improves a website's ranking in search engine results, leading to more visitors without paying for ads.
